Prompt Engineering

Code for this post can be found on my GitHub.

Have you ever wondered why the LLM you are using, say ChatGPT, provides a lot of additional information that isn't necessarily helpful and why certain other apps that use the same underlying LLM/GPT models provide more targeted responses (AnaystGPT for example)? One way we obtain more specific answers from our models is "prompt engineering". In this post I talk about prompt engineering from a practical perspective and share some insights.
