Fine-tuning LLMs

Introduction

In 1-2 years (if not much earlier), I expect that everyone will be running personalized AI assistants and image/video/music generator models either directly, or indirectly via other applications and tools, in their browser and iPhone.

TImho, this will lead to profound change in work and personal behaviors: you won't need to go to a search site, or look at a workflow queue, in your personal life you won't need to book a flight or go to a shopping site yourself; your personalized AI assistant will do this. You will simply get what you need when you need it.

There is always risk associated with new technology adoption and AI is no different. It is necessary to embrace AI in order to understand the risks and how to mitigate them. Sitting back and waiting simply means missing the boat. Fine-tuning an open-source LLM, such as Meta's LLaMA, is a great way to mitigate certain risks, so let's dive in and take a deeper look.
